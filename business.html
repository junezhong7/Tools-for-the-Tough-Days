The problem

"Traditional EAPs are designed for big companies."

Most EAP providers charge $30–80 per employee per year and require a minimum of 100+ staff. That locks out the vast majority of Australian businesses, and the workers who need support most.

"Supporting your people before they reach breaking point."

Most employees don't ask for help until things have become very serious. In over 21 years of clinical practice as a Registered Psychologist, that pattern is consistent, by the time someone walks through the door for counselling, the problem has been building for a long time.

The question for every business owner, HR manager, and leader isn't whether your people are struggling. It's whether they have somewhere to turn before it gets to that point.

That's exactly what Tools for the Tough Days is built for.

1 in 5 Australian workers experience a mental health condition each year. In trades and construction, suicide rates are 2× the national average. Your team deserves better than nothing.
