Your counselling sessions

3-session bundle - included in your plan

Your plan includes a bundle of 3 counselling sessions ($600 total) with a Registered Psychologist and Registered Counsellor. The 3 sessions are a shared pool - they can be used by one staff member or spread across the team, in any combination. There is no per-person limit.

1

Any staff member can book

Employees use the Calendly booking link within the platform to schedule a session at a time that suits them. No approval from you is needed or visible - they simply book and attend.

2

Sessions are held via phone or video

Sessions are conducted by phone or video call - whichever the team member prefers. Each session is typically 50 minutes.

3

You'll be notified when sessions are running low

When sessions are being drawn on, you'll receive a notification so you can track usage. You will never be told which team member attended - only how many sessions have been used from the bundle.

4

Top up with another bundle when needed

Once all 3 sessions have been used, you can purchase another 3-session bundle for $600 at any time. There's no obligation to purchase more - top up only when your team needs it.

Confidentiality is absolute. What a staff member discusses in a session is completely private - it is never reported to you, shared with the employer, or disclosed to any third party. Our practitioners operate under the same professional and ethical obligations as any registered health professional.
