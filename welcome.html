How to get the most from the platform
A few simple things that make a real difference to how much you benefit.

  • Start with your mood check-in

    Every time you visit, take the mood check-in honestly. It's not about picking a number - it's about letting the platform point you toward the right resources for today, not yesterday.

  • Read first, listen later ?or the other way around

    Every resource has both a written guide and an audio version. Try both and see what fits your day. The audio is great for commuting or when you can't sit with a screen.

  • Come back regularly - not just when things are bad

    The platform is most useful when it becomes part of your routine, not just a crisis resource. Even five minutes a week makes a difference over time.

  • Watch for new resources

    New content is added regularly. If you've opted into email updates, you'll hear when something new is added in an area relevant to you.

  • Book with Nic if you need more

    If you want to talk something through with a real person, a free 15-min call with Nic is always available. No pressure - it's just there if you need it.
